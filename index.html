WELCOME TO ESL 2025

The Egyptian Student Leaders Conference (ESL) brings together student government/associations leaders from universities across Egypt to learn ideas, build leadership capacity, and develop joint initiatives together from experienced individuals. Through talks, collaboration, and networking, ESL aims to strengthen student representation, promote cross-campus cooperation, and inspire impactful leadership across the country.

About ESL 2025

ESL 2025 will take place on November 21st, 2025 at the The American University in Cairo New Cairo campus. Join hundreds of student leaders and changemakers for a day of collaboration, workshops, and impactful sessions.

Organized by the AUC Student Union

The AUC Student Union (SU) is the highest representative body of the student community at the American University in Cairo. Serving as the primary bridge between students and administration, the SU strives to ensure that the voices, needs, and aspirations of the student body are heard and addressed. Rooted in the values of leadership, service, and inclusivity, the Student Union works to enhance the overall university experience by advocating for student rights, organizing impactful initiatives, and fostering a vibrant campus culture.

Beyond representation, the SU plays a central role in shaping student life at AUC through a variety of academic, social, and developmental programs. By empowering students to engage in governance, dialogue, and creative expression, the AUC Student Union continues to uphold its mission of cultivating a dynamic and unified community that reflects the diverse spirit of AUC.
