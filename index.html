WELCOME TO ESL 2025

The Egyptian Student Leaders Conference (ESL) brings together student government/associations leaders from universities across Egypt to learn ideas, build leadership capacity, and develop joint initiatives together from experienced individuals. Through talks, collaboration, networking, and policy innovation, ESG aims to strengthen student representation, promote cross-campus cooperation, and inspire impactful leadership across the country.

Recognition

About ESL 2025

ESL 2025 will take place on November 21st, 2025 at the American University in Cairo. Join hundreds of student leaders and changemakers for a day of collaboration, workshops, and impactful sessions.
